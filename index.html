According to the Canadian Government website as of September of 2024, there are 504 major projects under construction or planned for the next ten years. In the combined energy, forest, mining sectors this has the potential capital value of $632.6B. On the website we find that between 2023 and 2024, there were more projects added to the inventory than projects removed, leading to an increase in the overall project count. The demand for a central system to manage these major infrastructure projects in Canada is there and that were our company will create a robust, simple, easy to track system that will show progress, manage budgets, and ensure accountability.
